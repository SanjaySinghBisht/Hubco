All About Trademarks Filings in India

What is a Trademark?

Trademark is anything that describes your business, i.e. something that distinguish your business with others. It can be anything like Logo, mark, sound, badge, service mark, shape, pattern, colour, word, device, packaging etc.

How to register Trademark in India?

Registtering a Trademark in India is very simple but you need an expertise in this area. Here are the following steps you must follow before filing any trademark in India.

#Check Trademark Availability: The first step is to check whether your mark is available for registration under the respective class. There are 45 classes under Trademarks and hence class must be choosed appropriately.

#Prepare Documents for Trademark Filing: When you are getting ready for filing the logo/word/mark, you must prepare the application (TM 1) properly to avoid formality check failure later on

#File with Attorney Signature: Once your application is ready you must get it filed with the attorney.

Why to Register a Trademark?

Trademark registration gives you protection over your mark to claim the ownership on your brand. If you do not register your brand then someone else might steal your branding and ownership over your self generated goodwill and branding.

Requirements for Trademark Registration

There is mandatory requirement for Trademark filings prescribed. However, the three most important requirement are as follows:

  • - Extensive Research for brand/word/logo to avoid conflict later on with another trademark.
  • - Properly drafted application to avoid formality check failure later on.
  • - Regular check on the Trademark Status to avoid rejection of application.
